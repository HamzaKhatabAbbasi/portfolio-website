Who I Am

I'm Hamza Khatab, a passionate Data Analyst and Machine Learning enthusiast based in Islamabad, Pakistan. I thrive on transforming raw, complex datasets into meaningful insights that help businesses make informed, strategic decisions.

With a solid foundation in Business Analytics from the International Islamic University and advanced training in Data Science & AI from NUST University, I bring both academic rigor and hands-on expertise to every project I tackle.

My journey in data started with a fascination for patterns — how numbers can tell stories, predict outcomes, and uncover hidden truths. Today, I channel that curiosity into building predictive models, designing interactive dashboards, and crafting data-driven strategies that create real impact.

Bridge the gap between raw data and strategic decisions through clear, actionable insights.
